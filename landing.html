About 10-GAUSS

Welcome to 10-GAUSS!

We are proud to introduce 10-GAUSS, a dynamic and driven class from Bignay National High School, located in Valenzuela City. With a total of 47 students, we are guided by the leadership and dedication of our class adviser, Sir John Ryan Angel. You can find our classroom on the fourth floor, Room 401, where we build not just knowledge—but also friendships, discipline, and unity.
